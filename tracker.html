Kalkuletar Bibiyar Ciki

Mako na 12: Jariri girman plum ne

Ranar Haihuwa: 5 Janairu, 2025 (kwanaki 84 da suka rage)

Ci gaban Ciki

1st
2nd
3rd

Yanzu ana cikin zango (Trimester) ta farko - makonni 1-12

Jerin Ci gaban Jariri

Mako na 4: Irin Poppy

Girman jariri: kusan inci 0.078 (mm 2) tsayi

Nauyi: ƙasa da oz 0.04 (gram 1)

Muhimman Ci gaba:

  • Blastocyst ya shiga cikin bangon mahaifa
  • Placenta ya fara samuwa
  • Amniotic sac yana ci gaba
  • Tsarin jini na farko ya fara aiki

Shawara ta Lafiya

Fara shan kayan abinci masu gina jiki tare da folic acid don hana lahani ga jijiyoyin jiki.

Mako na 8: Rasberi

Girman jariri: kusan inci 0.63 (mm 16) tsayi

Nauyi: oz 0.04 (gram 1)

Muhimman Ci gaba:

  • Yatsun hannu da ƙafafu sun fara samuwa
  • Idanu da kunnuwa suna ci gaba
  • Za a iya gano bugun zuciya
  • Dukkan manyan gabobin jiki sun fara samuwa

Shawara ta Lafiya

Ku ci abinci kaɗan kaɗan sau da yawa don magance tashin zuciya na safe.

Mako na 12: Plum

Girman jariri: kusan inci 2.1 (cm 5.4) tsayi

Nauyi: oz 0.5 (gram 14)

Muhimman Ci gaba:

  • Hankali yana fara ci gaba
  • Yatsun hannu da ƙafafu sun rabu sosai
  • Idanu sun samu amma suna rufe
  • Ƙoda sun fara samar da fitsari

Shawara ta Lafiya

Ƙara yawan abinci mai gina jiki kamar omega-3 fatty acids (ana samun su a cikin kifi, gyada, da flaxseeds) don tallafawa ci gaban kwakwalwar jariri.

Mako na 16: Avocado

Girman jariri: kusan inci 4.6 (cm 11.6) tsayi

Nauyi: oz 3.5 (gram 100)

Muhimman Ci gaba:

  • Gashin kai yana fara samuwa
  • Kunnuwa kusan sun isa wurinsu na ƙarshe
  • Tsokoki suna ƙara ƙarfi
  • Za a iya fara jin motsi

Shawara ta Lafiya

Fara yin motsa jiki na ƙashin ƙugu (Kegels) don shirya haihuwa da hana rashin kula da fitsari.

Mako na 20: Ayaba

Girman jariri: kusan inci 6.5 (cm 16.4) tsayi

Nauyi: oz 10.6 (gram 300)

Muhimman Ci gaba:

  • Vernix caseosa (kariya) yana samuwa
  • Tsarin barci da farkawa yana aiki
  • Za a iya gano jinsin jariri
  • Motsi mai ƙarfi da za a iya gane shi

Shawara ta Lafiya

Kwana a gefenku (gwargwadon yiwuwa hagu) don inganta jigilar jini zuwa ga jariri. Yi amfani da matashin kai don tallafi.

Mako na 24: Masara

Girman jariri: kusan inci 8 (cm 20) tsayi

Nauyi: lb 1.3 (gram 600)

Muhimman Ci gaba:

  • Huhu yana ci gaba da sauri
  • Taste buds yana samuwa
  • Kwakwalwa tana girma da sauri
  • Fata tana zama ƙasa da canji

Shawara ta Lafiya

Ku lura da alamun haihuwa da wuri kamar ƙwanƙwasa na yau da kullun, matsa lamba a ƙashin ƙugu, ko zubar ruwa.

Mako na 28: Gauta

Girman jariri: kusan inci 10 (cm 25) tsayi

Nauyi: lb 2.2 (kg 1)

Muhimman Ci gaba:

  • Idanu suna iya buɗewa da rufewa
  • Kwakwalwa tana samar da biliyoyin neurons
  • Jariri yana iya mafarki yayin barci
  • Huhu yana iya numfashi

Shawara ta Lafiya

Yi allurar Tdap don kare jaririnku daga tari bayan haihuwa.

Mako na 32: Jicama

Girman jariri: kusan inci 11.4 (cm 29) tsayi

Nauyi: lb 3.75 (kg 1.7)

Muhimman Ci gaba:

  • Yana atisayen motsin numfashi
  • Ƙasusuwa sun cika amma har yanzu suna laushi
  • Fata tana zama ƙasa da ƙuƙumi
  • Barci kashi 90-95% na yini

Shawara ta Lafiya

Ku lura da motsin jariri kowace rana. Ku ba da rahoton duk wani raguwar motsi ga likitan ku.

Mako na 36: Lettuce

Girman jariri: kusan inci 12.5 (cm 32) tsayi

Nauyi: lb 5.75 (kg 2.6)

Muhimman Ci gaba:

  • Vernix yana fara ɓacewa
  • Jariri na iya zaune a matsayin kai ƙasa
  • Huhu sun kusan cika
  • Fata tana zama mai ruwan hoda da santsi

Shawara ta Lafiya

Ku shirya jakar asibiti da kuma kammala shirin haihuwa.

Mako na 40: Kabewa

Girman jariri: kusan inci 14 (cm 36) tsayi

Nauyi: lb 7.5 (kg 3.4)

Muhimman Ci gaba:

  • Ci gaban cikakken lokaci ya cika
  • Lanugo (gashi mai laushi) ya ƙare
  • Farce na iya wuce yatsun hannu
  • A shirye don rayuwa a wajen mahaifa

Shawara ta Lafiya

Ku san alamun haihuwa: ƙwanƙwasa na yau da kullun, ruwan ciki ya fashe, ko zubar jini. Ku tuntuɓi mai kula da ku lokacin da haihuwa ta fara.
